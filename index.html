GREAT BLUE SHARK

Blue sharks are sleek, pelagic predators, known for their vibrant blue coloration and long, pointed snout. They roam vast oceans globally.

THE BRAIN CORALS

Brain coral, a reef-building coral, forms large colonies resembling brain folds. Vital for reef ecosystems, they provide habitat and protection.

BELUGA WHALE

Beluga whales are Arctic mammals known for their white color and melon-shaped head. Highly social, they use echolocation to communicate.

FRINEDLY DOLPHINS

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals, known for their playful behavior, echolocation, and strong social bonds in ocean habitats.
